# Repeat Timers - Support

Thank you for using Repeat Timers! We designed this app to be as simple, reliable, and frictionless as possible. If you need help getting started or run into any issues, please review the FAQs below or contact us directly.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### How do I create a new timer?
Tap the large **"+"** button on the home screen. Set your desired **ON** and **OFF** durations using the scroll wheels, then tap **"Start"**. Your timer will immediately begin and will automatically be saved as a preset bubble on your home screen for next time.

### How do I delete a saved timer?
1. On the home screen, tap the **"Edit"** button.
2. Tap the red **"X"** icon on the timer preset you wish to delete.
3. Tap **"Done"** when finished.

### Does the timer work if I lock my device or switch apps?
Yes! You can start a timer and lock your device or switch to another app. Repeat Timers fully supports background execution. It will continue to track your intervals and notify you with distinct audio tones and haptic taps when it's time to transition.

### I can't hear the transition tones, what's wrong?
Ensure that your device is not muted or on Silent Mode (check the physical switch on your iPhone, or the Control Center on your Apple Watch). Also, make sure your device's media volume is turned up. The app relies on audio and haptic feedback to let you exercise without looking at your screen.

### Can I customize the colors or tones?
Currently, Repeat Timers uses a carefully designed high-contrast neon palette and distinct audio cues (ascending beeps for ON, descending beeps for OFF) that are scientifically formulated to be readable and audible during intense physical exertion. Customization may be added in a future update!
